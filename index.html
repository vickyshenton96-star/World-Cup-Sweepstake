🏆 Sweepstake Awards
⚽
Golden Boot Collector
Most goals scored · Group stage
Awarded to the player whose 4 group-stage teams combine for the highest total of goals scored. Every goal your teams put in the net counts — one prolific striker can swing this entirely. Calculated across all three group games for each of your four teams.
🪣
Sieve Award
Most goals conceded · Group stage
A dubious honour for the player whose 4 teams collectively leak the most goals. Whether it's a shaky goalkeeper, a high-line defence caught out repeatedly, or just plain bad luck — whoever racks up the most goals against across their teams takes this one home (in shame).
🟥
Bye Bye Bye
Most red cards · Group stage
The award no one wants. Given to the player whose 4 teams accumulate the most red cards across the group stage. Early baths, last-ditch lunges, off-the-ball incidents — it all adds up. The holder of this award can at least claim their teams played with passion.
