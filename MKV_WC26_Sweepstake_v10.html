🏆 Sweepstake Awards

Golden Boot Challenge
Most goals scored · Group stage
This is a team-based competition where the four teams you've drawn must work collectively to score as many goals as possible during the group stage. The participant whose teams accumulate the highest number of goals will take home the prize!
🪣
The Leaky Defence
Most goals conceded · Group stage
Think your teams aren't going to perform? This challenge might be for you! Your four teams will combine their efforts to concede as many goals as possible throughout the group stage. The participant whose teams let in the most goals wins the prize. It may feel like you're rooting against your own teams, but when there's a prize on the line, there are no friends at the top!
🟥
Bye Bye Bye
Most red cards · Group stage
The award no one wants. Given to the player whose 4 teams accumulate the most red cards across the group stage. Early baths, last-ditch lunges, off-the-ball incidents — it all adds up. The holder of this award can at least claim their teams played with passion.
⭐
Best Moment of the Cup
Determined by social media & media coverage
A moment that captured the tournament — a last-minute winner, a stunning upset, a wild celebration, or something completely unexpected. This award will be determined by social media engagement and media coverage. Factors such as likes, comments, shares, mentions, and overall news exposure will be considered to identify the player, team, or moment that captures the most attention throughout the tournament.
